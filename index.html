What is meded.ml?

Evaluation in Medical Education is difficult, full of competing priorities.
Should we focus on tracking progress? Or giving feedback?
Many short evaluations? Or a few detailed ones?

We believe careful application of Machine Learning (ML) and natural language processing (NLP) techniques can help solve these problems.
